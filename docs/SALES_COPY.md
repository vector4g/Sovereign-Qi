- **Run the simulation:** NVIDIA Omniverse-powered digital twins model stress, access, and risk across synthetic personas representing your most vulnerable populations

- **Alan (Turing) holds veto power:** If coded threats are detected (dog whistles, surveillance disguised as safety, identity-targeting patterns), Alan can BLOCK regardless of other votes

**What You Get:**

- "Curb-cut benefits"—improvements that help everyone, not just edge cases

### 1. We Test Before We Deploy

Every other governance tool is reactive. Wait for the complaint. Investigate the incident. Settle the lawsuit. Hope it doesn't happen again.
