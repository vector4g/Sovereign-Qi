## Synthetic Sovereignty

Marginalized communities are often “data poor” because visibility means risk. Sovereign Qi leverages Omniverse Replicator to generate synthetic personas that statistically mirror lived challenges—such as medical bias faced by a Black trans woman—without ever recording real individuals.[5][4]
